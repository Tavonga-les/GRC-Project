# 🛡 Governance & Risk (GRC)

This section demonstrates my ability to assess, manage, and mitigate organizational cybersecurity risks by applying industry-recognized frameworks and compliance standards such as **NIST SP 800-53**, **ISO 27001**, **PCI DSS**, **GDPR**, and **SOC 2**.

The projects included in this section reflect practical governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) activities aligned with real-world enterprise environments, including banking and financial services scenarios.

# 📄 Projects Included

## 1. Security Risk Assessment Report

### Goal

Identify, evaluate, and prioritize risks affecting organizational assets, systems, and operations.

### Highlights

* Developed a risk matrix using likelihood and impact ratings.
* Assessed threats, vulnerabilities, and business impacts.
* Prioritized mitigation strategies and residual risk handling.
* Applied concepts from the **NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)**.

### Key Skills Demonstrated

* Risk identification
* Risk analysis
* Risk prioritization
* Security recommendations
